How It Works

Find

Get alerts via email or WhatsApp when there is a car that shows up in our inventory. You can also search and add vehicles to your watchlist.

Bid & Buy

When the time comes to bid, you will get alerted to start the process. If the vehicle is available to buy now, then you can make an offer.

Track

We handle the transportation efforts and send you alerts on tracking the vehicle or vehicles.
